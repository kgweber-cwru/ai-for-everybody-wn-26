## Common Challenges & Solutions

### Challenge: Wide range of technical comfort
**Solution:** 
- Normalize asking questions
- Pair experienced with beginners
- Multiple pathways to success
- Celebrate all progress

### Challenge: Fear of AI replacing jobs
**Solution:**
- Address directly Week 1
- Emphasize augmentation not replacement
- Show how expertise remains critical
- Focus on enhanced capabilities

### Challenge: Concerns about accuracy
**Solution:**
- Week 4 covers this extensively
- Emphasize verification always
- Show how to fact-check
- Discuss appropriate use cases

### Challenge: Privacy worries
**Solution:**
- Week 4 addresses comprehensively
- Provide clear guidelines
- Show de-identification techniques
- Know institutional policies

### Challenge: Keeping up with changes
**Solution:**
- Teach principles, not just tools
- Emphasize adaptability
- Create learning community
- Plan update sessions
