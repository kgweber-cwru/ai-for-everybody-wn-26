### Slides 4-8: Advanced Prompting Techniques

**Chain-of-Thought:**
- "Let's think step by step"
- Improves complex reasoning
- Demo: Math problem with/without CoT

**Self-Consistency:**
- Generate multiple solutions
- Compare approaches
- Increases reliability

**Verification Prompts:**
- "Check your work"
- "What could be wrong?"
- "What assumptions did you make?"

### Slides 9-14: Hallucinations & Fact-Checking

**What are hallucinations?**
- Plausible but false information
- Made-up citations
- Why: Prediction not knowledge

**Red Flags:**
- Unverifiable specifics
- Missing citations
- Contradicts known facts

**Verification Strategies:**
- Cross-reference claims
- Check citations independently
- Use multiple sources
- Apply domain expertise

**Reducing Hallucinations:**
- "Only verifiable information"
- "Cite sources"
- "If uncertain, say so"

### Slides 15-19: Ethics & Bias

**Ethical Considerations:**
- Disclosure when appropriate
- Academic integrity
- Professional standards
- Patient care ethics

**Understanding Bias:**
- Training data reflects society
- Can perpetuate stereotypes
- Awareness crucial

**Mitigation:**
- Request multiple perspectives
- Question assumptions
- Critical evaluation
- Diverse examples

---

### Slides 20-22: Privacy & Security

**Never Share:**
- Patient information (PHI/PII)
- Confidential data
- Proprietary information
- Sensitive research data

**Best Practices:**
- De-identify before using
- Check privacy policies
- Use secure instances when available
- Follow institutional guidelines
